"Technology is evolving at such a breakneck speed that it appears to be moving faster than light! This week's hottest technology or programming language may become obsolete in the next few days! As more money is put into Research and Development, computer scientists and professionals are continually refining and upgrading existing technologies to make the most of it.Here are some of the latest trending technologies that are sure to dominate the IT industry in 2021 and the upcoming years

1. Artificial Intelligence (AI): Artificial intelligence (AI) is a technology that allows computers to make judgments in the same way that people do. When AI programs are given to systems, the goal is to replicate human intelligence for difficult tasks such as pattern identification, speech recognition, weather forecasting, and medical diagnosis, to name a few.AI automation is anticipated to provide over 70 million jobs by 2030. The sad reality is that AI might eliminate over 23 million employment in the same time frame. Jobs in fields such as testing, support, maintenance, programming, and data science will be created as a result of AI. Software developers who are familiar with AI earn far more than those who are not.

2. Virtual Reality (VR): Virtual reality (VR) is a technology that allows you to immerse yourself in an astoundingly realistic environment. It is the creation of a simulated world using computer technology. It is often used to play video games on computers. You are directly put in the gaming world, unlike traditional games where you experience the gaming environment by seeing it on a screen. These surroundings replicate the senses of touch, hearing, smell, and vision. You can move around and play the game in that 3D world using VR equipment such as headsets. The technology utilized to improve this virtual environment is called augmented reality (AR).

3. Data Science ( Data Science is, unsurprisingly, next on the list of the most recent technological topics. Data Science is a technology that aids in the interpretation of complex data. Companies produce a massive amount of data every day, as you are aware. This comprises business information, sales statistics, customer profiles, server information, and financial details. The majority of this information comes in the form of large, unstructured data sets. Data scientists are responsible for converting these unstructured data sets into structured datasets. These data sets can then be examined for patterns and trends. These trends can help you better understand your company's business performance, customer retention, and how you may enhance these areas.
